Our Craftsmanship Story

Three generations of passion, precision, and dedication to Nigerian furniture making excellence

Heritage of Excellence Since 1985

FurnitureMan began as a small workshop in Lagos in 1985, founded by master craftsman Chinedu Okoro. What started as a passion for woodworking has grown into a renowned furniture design and manufacturing company across Nigeria, while maintaining our commitment to quality and tradition.

Today, led by Chinedu's son Emeka and granddaughter Adaora, we continue to honor time-honored techniques while embracing innovative designs and sustainable practices. Each piece we create tells a story of dedication, artistry, and the enduring beauty of fine Nigerian craftsmanship.

Our workshop in Ikeja employs over 50 skilled artisans who bring decades of experience to every project. We source our materials responsibly from sustainable Nigerian forests, using Iroko, Mahogany, Obeche, and African Walnut to create furniture that's beautiful, durable, and proudly Nigerian.
